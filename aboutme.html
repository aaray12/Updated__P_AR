About Me

I am a recent graduate of the North Western coding bootcamp where I learned full stack web development. Thought the course we covered HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, React.js, Express.js and more.

Originally I graduated from DePaul University where I studied communications and media production with a minor in film. Throughout college and after, I had a number of interesting jobs ranging from a production for Ravinia, visitor services for the MCA, and most recently I worked as an operations coordinator for a local logistic company.

While these are all very different jobs in their own right, experiencing them and being exposed to the different work dynamics has helped me develop skills for problem solving in creative ways. And it is the problem solving that I love most about programming. Starting with a complicated problem, breaking it down, and following each bit information to reach a resolution, like a modern day Sherlock Homes, is as exiting as it gets!
